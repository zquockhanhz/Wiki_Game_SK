Living Room

In the Living Room, there are many different objects and features the player can interact with for different actions:

  • Choose characters.
  • Door - To starts the game.
  • Chest - Unlocked by a one-time purchase of 1,000 gems.
  • Mail Guy - The player will receive 500 gems when interacting with him.
  • Email - Used to collect Achievement rewards.
  • TV - Gives 20 gold after watching an advertisement video.
  • Safe - Increases the starting amount of gold coins. Upgradable by using gems.
  • Badass Travel - Switches the difficulty into Badass Mode upon interaction.
  • Fish Tank - May give a random fish weapon.

Garden

The Garden is an area located to the left of the Living Room.


It includes eight Garden Plots; three empty and usable, and five unlockable. It also has a Shovel, a Watering Can, the current amount of Fertilizer, a Pet the player possesses and a Training Dummy at bottom of the garden and a Magic Well after it has been developed.


  • Garden plots can be used to plant seeds the player has obtained.
  • There is a pet at the left side of the Garden.
  • Once the player has obtained seeds, the player can plant them in an empty plot.
  • A plant can't be harvested twice on the same run.

Work Shop

The Workshop is an area located to the right of the Living Room.


In this room, the player can buy Materials, forge weapons and Armor Mounts, unlock new weapons to forge from the Design Table.


  • Computer - There is a damaged Robot connected to it.
  • Forge Table - A weapon obtained at least 8 times will appear on the list of weapons. Any forged weapon will appear on the table, so that the player can grab it.
  • Trader - The Trader will place a new item after the player has bought something (up to 5 times a day).
  • Renovation Project Leader - Used for changing the Multiplayer waiting room skin.
  • Redeem Machine - Use vouchers to get random items of corresponding type.
  • Robotics Facility - Use materials and gems to create Armor Mounts to bring to a run.
