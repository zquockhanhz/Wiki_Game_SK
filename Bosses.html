Bosses

Bosses are strong enemies with high damage and hp found on the 5th level of each floor (levels x-5). Based on the current biome, Bosses are chosen at random.

After defeating a Boss in level mode, a Golden Chest will spawn. This does not occur at 3-5, as there is no point in giving the player a weapon after the game is over, unless when the player has Loop Mode or Random levels challenge.

The summoned minions of the Boss can be alive after its death. This can happen if the boss dies before the minions appear from the summoning attack, as any minions already summoned will die with the boss.

If the Boss has less than half of total HP, it will become enraged and will start to use more powerful attacks, as well as attacking much faster and more aggressively. In addition, Enraged Bosses will recover from debuffs faster than usual. At half of its total HP and upon death, they will also drop 10 energy orbs for players to recover. Upon death, they will drop a lot of Gold, materials and energy.
