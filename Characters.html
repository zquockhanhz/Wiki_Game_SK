Characters

Characters are playable heroes and can be controlled by the player in-game.

There are currently 24 playable characters, in which 5 of them are Legendary Heroes.

Different characters have a unique set of stats, skills and starter weapons (see table below). Some characters can be purchased using in-game gems, while others can only be acquired by purchasing them with real money.

The player can select a character by tapping on them in the Living Room after starting a game. Each character has their own set of skins, which are obtainable through different methods.

​​​​​Levelling up a character gives the following bonuses:

  • 1 star: Permanent +1 health (cost: 500 gems)
  • 2 stars: Permanent +1 armor (cost: 1,000 gems)
  • 3 stars: Permanent +20 energy (cost: 1,500 gems)
  • 4 stars: Reduces skill cooldown by 2 seconds (cost: 2,000 gems)
  • 5 stars: Skill upgrade - Different effects for each character and skill. Affects all skills for a single upgrade (cost: 2,500 gems)
  • 6 stars: The character obtains a permanent buff that complements their skills/play style (cost: 5,000 gems)
  • 7 stars: Permanently upgrades the character's starter weapon (cost: 8,000 gems)
  • 8 stars: Gives the applicable material to develop the respective Summoner's Artifact (cost: 10,000 gems)
